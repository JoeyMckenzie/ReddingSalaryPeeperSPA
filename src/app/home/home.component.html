Redding Salary Peeper

What is Redding Salary Peeper?

Salary Peeper is a consolidation of publicly available salary data for both the City of Redding and Shasta County, with the purpose of providing the denizens of Redding a platform for transparency into the designation of budgeted salaries for both agencies. Salary Peeper is an analogous project to Budget Peeker, an open source application that provides financial data for the the City of Sacramento's approved budget from fiscal years 2015 to 2018.

Per the mission objective of Transparent California's site:

"Transparent California is provided by the Nevada Policy Research Institute as a public service and is dedicated to providing accurate, comprehensive and easily searchable information on the compensation of public employees in California. Complete and accurate information is necessary to increase public understanding of government and help decision makers, including elected officials and voters, make informed decisions."

Mission statement from Transparent California

The goal of Salary Peeper is to provide a simple web tool for the people of Redding, improving upon Transparent California's existing data warehouse for salary information.

The Data?

Salary Peeper sources its data from Transparent California, an open data portal for salary information of the public sector within the state of California. Data is sorted by various pay types, agencies, fiscal year, and job titles.
